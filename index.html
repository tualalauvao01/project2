Why choose Blockout

Blockout is a handy gadget that will allow you to enjoy your privacy. Say goodbye to the sticky tape that you have to constantly replace when it has lost its stickiness and say hello to a gadget that will come to be useful for you. Blockout can help you fight the fear of spies, or the government, watching you. For you university students who are super sceptical about spies, this Blockout gadget is for you!

Have you heard of, or read of, Cambridge Analytica? This group used facebook in order to gain votes for the US elections for Donald Trump. If they can do this, who knows how many other groups or people out there can have access to your webcam, to your data and to everything you digitally own online. Blockout is one step forward in trying to help protect and prevent creepy spies watching you without your consent or knowledge.

Functionality & Purpose

The function of this gadget is to block out the spies that watch us through our laptop cameras. I have seen plenty of university students who have sticky tape covering their cameras which led to this idea of having something to replace the sticky tape. The puropose is simple and has been mentioned- it is to block out those sneaky hacker spies who enjoy creepily watching you without your knowledge.

The aim with this product is to keep those nasty spies from secretly, and creepily, watching you while you do your school work, watch your movies, or whatever you choose to do on your laptop.

The reason for having the option to reveal your camera is for those who take video calls, whether that be business or family. This makes it easier for the user as they don't have to rip off a piece of sticky tape and put it back on after it has lost its "stickiness".

Packaging and Material.

The inspiration for the packaging of this device comes from USB packaging. I was thinking of havinga sliding wooden box for the productto sit in. The sliding motion being continued throughout the whole design. Inspired by this image, a wooden box package for Branded USB Power. For the packaging we also want something that can last and be more sustainable for our university student target audience.

We want the sliding motion, but we dont’ want the sliding piece to come out fully, because otherwise it could get lost. The material will definitely be thicker, using MDF wood so it won’t be this thin. For the sliding piece to stay in placeand not slide out on its own when tipped, the area inside would be tight enough for it to slide but not too tight where it can’t move at all.

Inspired by website Branded USB Power
